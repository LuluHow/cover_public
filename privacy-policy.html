Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

Welcome to COVER. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

COVER does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play the game. We do not collect:

  • Name or email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information
  • Social media profiles

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use COVER, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game progress, levels completed, time spent in app
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs for debugging purposes

1.3 Local Storage

All game progress (completed levels, unlocked levels, best times) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Advertising

COVER uses third-party advertising services to display ads. These services may collect information about your device and usage to serve relevant ads. We use:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads

These advertising partners may use technologies such as cookies and device identifiers to collect information. Please refer to their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps

2.2 Analytics (If Applicable)

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with COVER. This helps us improve the game. Analytics data is anonymous and aggregated.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Game Functionality: To save your progress and preferences locally
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through third-party ad networks
  • App Improvement: To identify bugs, crashes, and improve performance
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve gameplay

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • Ad Networks: Google AdMob for serving advertisements
  • Analytics Providers: For understanding app usage (if applicable)

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred.

4.4 What We Do NOT Do

We will NEVER:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your game progress with others
  • Use your data for purposes other than those stated in this policy

5. Children's Privacy

COVER is rated for ages 4+ and is appropriate for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

The app does not:

  • Require registration or personal information
  • Contain social features or chat
  • Allow sharing of personal information

Parents, if you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at contact@ludovichorem.com.

6. Ad Choices and Opt-Out

You have choices regarding ads:

  • iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Note: Opting out will not reduce the number of ads you see, but ads may be less relevant to you.

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Game data is stored locally on your device with iOS/Android security
  • We use secure connections when communicating with ad networks
  • We regularly update the app to fix security vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

Your local game data (progress, levels, times) is stored on your device indefinitely until you:

  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear app data manually
  • Reset your device

We do not store this data on our servers, so we cannot restore it if deleted.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (uninstall removes all local data)
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising (see section 6)

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@ludovichorem.com.

10. International Data Transfers

If you use COVER from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries. These countries may have different data protection laws.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, email contact@ludovichorem.com.

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Legal basis for processing: Legitimate interests (game functionality), Consent (ads)

13. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective when posted. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • An in-app notification
  • An update to the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use of COVER after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Third-Party Links

COVER may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us:

Support: contact@ludovichorem.com

Website: www.covergame.com

16. Consent

By using COVER, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
