üéÆ Gameplay

How do I play COVER?

COVER is a path puzzle game where you must:

  1. Tap the starting door (colored door matching the first color in the sequence)
  2. Swipe through adjacent cells to draw your path
  3. Pass through colored doors in the correct order
  4. Visit EVERY cell on the grid exactly once
  5. Complete the path to win!
What are the colored doors?

Colored doors must be passed through in a specific order. Look at the color sequence at the top of the screen - you must pass through doors in that exact order. If you pass through a door out of sequence, you won't be able to complete the level.

What are walls and how do they work?

Walls (shown as thick lines between cells) block your path. You cannot pass through walls. You must find a path around them to complete the level.

How does the Back Swipe Mode work?

When you fill 50% of the grid, you can activate Back Swipe Mode by tapping the gauge. This gives you 15 seconds to swipe backwards and erase mistakes. Use it wisely!

üí° Hints & Help

How does the hint system work?

Your first hint is FREE! After that, you can watch a short ad to get additional hints. Hints show you up to 20 cells of the solution path, helping you progress when you're stuck.

I'm stuck on a level. What should I do?

Try these strategies:

  • Use the hint button (lightbulb icon) to see part of the solution
  • Reset the level and try a different path
  • Look for cells that only have one adjacent free cell - these must be at the start or end of your path
  • Plan your route through doors before you start
  • Take a break and come back with fresh eyes!

How do I turn off the music?

Tap the volume icon in the top-right corner during gameplay to toggle music on/off. Your preference is saved automatically.

Can I play offline?

Yes! COVER can be played completely offline. Internet is only required for watching ads to get hints.

üì± Technical Issues

The app is crashing. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Close the app completely and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store/Play Store
  4. Free up storage space on your device (app needs ~50MB)
  5. If the problem persists, contact us at contact@ludovichorem.com
The game is running slowly or lagging

Performance tips:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure your device meets minimum requirements (iOS 12+ / Android 6+)
  • Turn off other animations in your device settings
I lost my progress after updating

Your progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstalled/reinstalled the app, progress may be lost. Unfortunately, we cannot restore lost progress at this time. We recommend not uninstalling the app to preserve your progress.

Ads are not loading for hints

If ads aren't loading:

  • Make sure you have an internet connection
  • Try again in a few moments
  • Remember: Your first hint is always free!
  • Check your ad tracking settings in device privacy settings
