How to Play

1

Tap the Starting Door

Begin your path by tapping on the colored door that matches the first color in the sequence.

2

Swipe Through Cells

Draw your path by swiping through adjacent cells. Each cell can only be visited once.

3

Pass Through Doors

Navigate through colored doors in the correct order while avoiding walls.

4

Cover Every Cell

Complete the level by visiting every single cell on the grid exactly once.
